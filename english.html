The Japan symposium on commutative algebra started in 1978 as a forum for presenting and exchanging information on the latest research results on commutative algebra. We will hold the symposium this year as follows.

  • Date: November 20 - 24 (Thu-Mon), 2025
  • Place: FUKURACIA
  • Invited speakers: Aldo Conca (University of Genova)
                                   Somayeh Moradi (Ilam University)
                                   Uwe Nagel (University of Kentucky)
